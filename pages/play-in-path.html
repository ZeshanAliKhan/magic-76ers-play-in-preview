Play-In path

What the Magic 76ers Play-In game means.

The NBA schedule listing labels Orlando at Philadelphia as the East game where the winner earns the 7 seed. That makes the prize immediate even though the loser is not eliminated.

If Orlando wins

The Magic take the road win and move directly into the East playoff bracket as the No. 7 seed. That also means avoiding the second Play-In game.

If Philadelphia wins

The 76ers protect home court and claim the No. 7 seed. For Philadelphia, that is the cleanest path because it avoids turning the week into another pressure game.

If either team loses

The loser still has another chance for the No. 8 seed against the winner of the East 9-vs-10 game. The risk is simple: another game means another injury report, another quick scout, and another elimination-level night.
